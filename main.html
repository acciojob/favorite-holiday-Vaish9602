Maldives


The Maldives is a tropical paradise known for its crystal-clear waters, white sandy beaches, and luxurious overwater villas. It is one of the most relaxing and beautiful destinations in the world.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Maafushi Island – famous for its water sports and vibrant local life.
  2. Vaadhoo Island – known for the magical "Sea of Stars" phenomenon.
  3. Banana Reef – one of the best diving spots in the Maldives.
  4. Male Friday Mosque – a cultural and historical landmark
  5. Hulhumalé Beach – perfect for peaceful sunsets and relaxation.

Must-Try Foods

  • Mas Huni – a traditional Maldivian breakfast dish.
  • Garudhiya – a flavorful fish broth served with rice.
  • Fihunu Mas – spicy grilled fish with local seasonings.
  • Hedhikaa – a variety of Maldivian tea-time snacks.
  • Saagu Bondibai – a sweet sago pudding.
